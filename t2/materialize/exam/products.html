We are located in the small town of La Revilla, a beautiful town in the epicenter of the Oyambre Natural Park and just 4 kilometers from San Vicente de la Barquera and its fabulous beaches.

Once here you will be able to enjoy the particularity of our environment, highlighting the natural contrast of having the sea and mountains so close. You can relax walking along the shores of the Cantabrian Sea, on any of the beaches that surround us, watching in the background how the mountains of the majestic Picos de Europa rise or approach the "Mirador de la Pita" to obtain a panoramic view of the Ría of San Vicente, the Rubín marshes and the Picos de Europa in the background.

Come with us, enjoy nature, the gastronomy of the area and an endless number of activities that you can do as a family, as a couple or with friends (surfing, walking routes, horseback riding, golf, canoeing, etc...).
